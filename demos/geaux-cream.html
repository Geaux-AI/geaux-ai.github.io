About us

What we do

Geaux AI is a small consulting and automation practice. We help people put AI to work without the guesswork, and without buying tools they do not need.

You bring a task that eats your time. We figure out what it really is, pick the smallest thing that solves it, build it, and hand it over so you own it. No lock-in, no jargon, and no pressure to automate what is fine as it is.

Our approach

Diagnose first. Recommend second.

Think of us like any good service call. You tell us what is wrong, we look before we quote, and we only do the work that fixes it. The order matters, so the problem always comes before the tool.
