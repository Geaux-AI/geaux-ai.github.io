Our Mission
Help student organizations strategically implement AI to optimize their workflows — ethically, intentionally, and effectively.

Every student org runs on repetitive work — reporting, records, scheduling, reviews. Most of it can be streamlined with AI, but only when it's done deliberately, around how your organization actually operates.

We start with a conversation, not a tool. We map your workflow, find where AI genuinely helps, and build or train around it — then hand it off so your members own it, not us.

What We Do

Three ways we help

Every engagement starts with your workflow, not our tools.

01

Workflow Discovery

We sit down with your leadership, map how work actually gets done, and identify where AI genuinely helps — and just as importantly, where it doesn't.

02

Custom AI Tools

Where a real tool is warranted, we design and build it — scoped to your organization and calibrated to LSU standards, not generic templates.

03

Training & Enablement

We show your members what they can do with the AI accounts they already have — turning their real problems into working workflows they run themselves.

The Agent Staff

Five specialized AI agents, each built and supervised by our human team, handle the operational load of every engagement. They're tools, not members — but they're how a two-person team delivers like a firm of ten.

Scout

Discovery

Maps a client org's workflows from intake questionnaires and meeting notes, and surfaces the bottlenecks worth automating first.

Scribe

Documentation

Turns meetings into minutes, drafts reports and records, and keeps every engagement's documentation current and organized.

Ledger

Data & Finance

Cleans spreadsheets, reconciles budgets, and builds the recurring reports that org treasurers redo by hand every week.

Coach

Career Materials

Reviews resumes against the LSU Olinde Career Center rubric with bullet-level feedback — the engine behind our resume review tool.

Relay

Communications

Drafts outreach, follow-ups, and member communications, and keeps scheduling coordinated across every active engagement.
