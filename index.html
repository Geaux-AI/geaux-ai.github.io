The Problem

Every LSU student org faces the same bottleneck

Different missions, same admin chaos. Here's what we see across campus:

🏫 Academic Clubs

Event sign-ups that nobody tracks

RSVP lists live in email threads. Attendance gets marked manually (or not at all). No one knows who actually showed up to what.

💼 Professional Societies

Dues collection is a nightmare

Treasurer tracks who paid in a spreadsheet. Reminders go out sporadically. Accounting for who owes what doesn't reconcile. Transfers to national never balance.

🎯 Business Clubs

Point systems live in someone's head

Event attendance, volunteer hours, committee work all feed into points — but calculations are manual, inconsistent, and lost when officers graduate.

🏆 Honor Societies

Member eligibility tracking is tribal

Who meets the GPA? Who completed the service hours? Which seniors get awards? The answers live in one person's memory, not a system.

🤝 Social & Community Orgs

Communications fragment across platforms

Event details in GroupMe, meeting notes in Google Docs, rosters in email, announcements in Instagram. Nothing stays in sync.

🎨 Greek Life & Fraternities

Officer transitions lose everything

Every semester, crucial workflows, preferences, and procedures disappear. The new treasurer has to reinvent dues tracking from scratch.

Our Solution

Automation that actually fits your org

We build custom workflows for your specific organization, your tools, your rules — then hand it off so you own it forever.

Geaux AI automates the repetitive, manual work that eats hours every week: dues reminders and reconciliation, attendance logging and eligibility tracking, event RSVPs and coordination, point system calculations, member communications.

Every solution is built specifically for your org — your LSU Student Involvement compliance, your national bylaws, your treasury tools, your preferences. Not a template. Not a generic SaaS product. Custom.

And every client gets a permanent memory file, so your automation preferences, tool choices, and procedures survive officer transitions. Next year's treasurer doesn't have to reinvent the wheel.

How We Work

Plain and simple

No black-box AI running your org. You get a working system built with you, tested by your officers, and documented so any future board can run it.

1

We talk

A real conversation with your board about what's eating your time every week. Free, no pitch.

2

We build it

We set it up in the tools you already use. AI helps us work fast — it never sends, charges, or changes anything on its own.

3

You approve it

Your officers test everything and sign off before launch. If something needs Campus Life or national approval, we flag it — the call is always yours.

4

We stay with you

Training for your board, fixes when something breaks, and a plain-English guide your next exec board inherits. You own it — no subscription, no lock-in.

The Swarm

Nine specialized AI agents — one for every seat on the average LSU exec board. Agents draft, organize, and track. People approve. Nothing touches money, member data, or messages without a human saying so.

The President Agent

Executive Oversight

Keeps the whole org visible: meeting agendas, project status ledgers, deadline tracking, and delegation follow-ups — so nothing lives in one person's head.

The Vice President Agent

Internal Operations

Coordinates committees and builds officer transition documents, so every procedure survives graduation and the next board doesn't start from scratch.

The Treasurer Agent

Finances & Dues

Dues collection workflows, reminder messages ready for your treasurer to send, event budgets, and Venmo/QuickBooks/Google Sheets ledgers you can reconcile in minutes.

The Secretary Agent

Records & Minutes

Meeting minutes formatted and filed, rosters kept current, attendance logged automatically, and member correspondence templated and consistent.

The Events Chair Agent

Programming

RSVP tracking that actually works, day-of run sheets, venue and catering checklists, and post-event attendance synced straight to your points ledger.

The Marketing Chair Agent

PR & Communications

Announcement blasts across GroupMe, Instagram, and email from one draft. Content calendars, flyer copy, and a consistent voice for your org.

The Recruitment Chair Agent

Membership Growth

Interest form pipelines, automatic follow-ups with prospective members, rush event coordination, and onboarding sequences for new members.

The Service Chair Agent

Philanthropy & Service

Volunteer hour tracking per member, fundraiser ledgers that reconcile, and outreach templates for community partners and sponsors.

The Risk Manager Agent

Compliance & Eligibility

Helps your officers spot the right questions to ask — LSU guidelines, national bylaws, data privacy — and keeps member eligibility (GPA, hours, standing) organized in one place. Decisions stay with your board.
