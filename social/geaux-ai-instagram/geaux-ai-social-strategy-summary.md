# 3. STRATEGY TEARDOWN: 4 WINNING PATTERNS GEAUX AI SHOULD COPY

### Pattern 1: Proof First, Pitch Second
**What top creators do:** Lead every post with a concrete result, real numbers, or real client story. The pitch comes last (or not at all).

**Example from research:** @theaiagents: "We saved this agency 40 hours/month by automating their intake process. Here's how."

**Why it works:** Stops the scroll (specificity + credibility), builds trust before asking anything, feels like teaching, not selling.

**GEAUX AI application:** Every Monday = proof post with real numbers (case study). Lead with "$2k saved" or "6 hours freed up per week."

---

### Pattern 2: Permission-Giving + Actionable
**What top creators do:** Remove the barrier to entry. "You don't have to code," "This takes 2 minutes," "No tool required." Then give exact steps.

**Example from research:** @digicuratorsocialai: "5 AI tools for small business (you can start today with free versions)"

**Why it works:** Solopreneurs are overwhelmed and scared. Permission-giving + step-by-step = they actually try it. Saves/shares explode.

**GEAUX AI application:** Every Tuesday & Sunday = Quick-Win posts with exact step counts ("3-minute fix," "60-second automation"). Remove jargon. Permission to try.

---

### Pattern 3: Teach ONE Thing Per Post, Deeply
**What top creators do:** Pick one concept, one tool, one process, one mistake. Go deep. Don't spray 5 ideas at once.

**Example from research:** Focus on "why automation fails" (one reason, well-explained) vs "5 reasons automation fails."

**Why it works:** Algorithmic (focused intent = clear topic signal), memorable (one idea sticks), actionable (specific enough to act on).

**GEAUX AI application:** Wednesday/Thursday/Friday posts = pick one concept, one tool comparison, one workflow. Frame it with a question or contrarian take. Deep dive.

---

### Pattern 4: Cadence + Consistency > Viral Hits
**What top creators do:** Post regularly (3–5 per week, sometimes daily). Show up reliably. Build community over time, not one-off viral posts.

**Engagement data:** Accounts with under 50K followers but 10%+ engagement = posting regularly + authentic + responsive to comments.

**Why it works:** Algorithm rewards consistent, original creators. Followers expect you. Community builds trust and retention.

**GEAUX AI application:** Commit to 7 posts per week (one per day) for 4 weeks. After 4 weeks, measure what works. Rotate in new examples but keep the same cadence.

To keep this calendar running *without* you managing it daily:

1. **Meta Business Suite** (free, built into Instagram)
   - Batch schedule all 7 posts Sunday evening

2. **Later or Buffer** (if you want advanced analytics)
   - Schedule + get engagement reports
   - See which posts perform best
   - Adjust next week's content based on data
   - Cost: ~$15/mo | Time saved: 45 mins/week + data insights

### Medium: Engagement Tracking

**What to measure (each week):**
- Saves (most important, means people want to reference it later)
- Shares (second, means they want to send it to someone)
- Comments (community signal; less important than saves)
- Link clicks (if you're driving to a landing page)

**Weekly 15-minute audit:**
1. Screenshot top 3 posts from the week
2. Note what format/pillar worked
3. Double down on that pattern next month

### Long-term: Repurpose This Content

Each Instagram post can become:
- **TikTok video** (same hook + slightly faster pacing)
- **LinkedIn post** (formalize the tone, add professional framing)
- **Email newsletter segment** (expand the caption, add data)

# 8. NOTES ON VOICE & TONE

Every post follows these rules:

- **Practical, not inspirational:** We teach you to do the work. Not motivational poster energy.
- **Plain English, zero jargon:** "An agent reads invoices" not "leverage autonomous systems for RPA orchestration"
- **Encouraging but honest:** "You can do this" + "here's why it's hard"
- **Anti-perfectionism:** Messy > perfect. Ship it and improve.
- **Solopreneur-first:** We understand you're one person doing many jobs. We speak to that.
