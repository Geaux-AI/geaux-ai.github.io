## VOICE RULES (Never Violate)

- Plain English (no jargon like "RPA," "orchestration," "leverage")
- Specific over vague ("$2k saved" not "significant savings")
- One idea per post, go deep
- Actionable steps (if teaching, give exact steps)
- Solopreneur-first (acknowledge they're one person doing many things)
- Encouraging but honest (realistic about hard parts)

---

## SCHEDULING WORKFLOW (5 mins on Sunday)

1. Open Meta Business Suite or Later
2. Queue all 7 captions (copy-paste from above or full plan)
3. Attach graphics (9-11 ratio for Instagram grid)
4. Set times: 9:00 AM ET Mon–Sun
5. Schedule
6. Done. No daily posting.

---

## ENGAGEMENT WORKFLOW (Daily, 10 mins)

**First 2 hours after post:**
- Reply to all comments (algorithm signal)
- Like/reply to replies (build community)
- Ask a question or pin a comment

**Friday morning:**
- Screenshot top 3 posts
- Note which pillar/format won (saves > shares > comments)
- Plan next week based on what worked

---

## WHAT WORKS (Ranked by Engagement)

1. **Proof/Wins** (saves + shares highest)
2. **Quick-Win Actions** (comments + engagement highest)
3. **Education, Contrarian** (shares + controversy highest)
4. **Behind-the-Scenes** (saves + curiosity highest)
5. **Tools/Stack** (saves highest, medium comments)
